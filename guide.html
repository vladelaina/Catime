Getting Started

No installation required. When opened for the first time, a 25-minute countdown will appear at the top.

Simple Portable

Tip

It is recommended to drag the Catime tray icon from the hidden area to the taskbar for easier access.

Basic Operations

All operations in Catime are done by clicking the taskbar icon:

Left Click

Open Time Management Interface

Right Click

Open Settings Menu

Customization

My friend, I believe you can't wait to move the window position, right? For example, moving it to the bottom left corner of the screen. At this time, simply Right-click the tray icon and select "Edit Mode". Then, the window will turn into an acrylic background, and you can drag with the mouse to adjust the position, and use the scroll wheel to adjust the size. After adjustment, right-click on the window to close Edit Mode~
